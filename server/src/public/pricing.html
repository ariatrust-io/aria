Professional & Business: your service never stops. Events beyond the base limit continue normally and are billed as overage at the end of the month.
If you exceed your monthly base limit, each additional 100,000 events is charged at your plan's overage rate: $1.00/100K for Professional and $0.75/100K for Business. For example: a Business customer who uses 5.3M events pays $149 + (300K / 100K × $0.75) = $151.25 that month. Your agents never go offline.
Both plans include the full ARIA feature set. The difference is scale: Professional supports up to 5 agents and 500K events/month, ideal for startups with a few production agents. Business supports up to 20 agents and 5M events/month, designed for teams running heavy workloads like customer service bots, data pipelines, or multi-agent orchestrators. Business also gets a lower overage rate ($0.75 vs $1.00 per 100K).
