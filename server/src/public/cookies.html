Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026  ·  Effective: May 19, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website when you visit it. They are widely used to make websites work correctly and to provide information to site owners.

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies ARIA uses, why we use them, and how you can control them.

2. Cookies We Use

ARIA uses the minimum number of cookies necessary to operate the Service. We do not use cookies for advertising, analytics tracking, or any third-party profiling.

aria_session
Essential — Session authentication. This cookie keeps you logged in to the ARIA Dashboard. It contains an encrypted session token that identifies your account. Without this cookie, you would be required to log in on every page load.

Duration: session (expires when you close the browser, or after your session timeout).
Set by: ariatrust.org
Data sent to: ariatrust.org only

ARIA sets no other first-party cookies. Specifically, we set none of the following:

  • No analytics cookies (no Google Analytics, Mixpanel, or similar)
  • No advertising cookies (no retargeting, no ad networks)
  • No social media tracking pixels
  • No fingerprinting or supercookies

3. Third-Party Checkout Cookies

When you visit the Pricing page or the landing page, a script from Lemon Squeezy (lemon.js) is loaded to enable the in-page checkout overlay. If you interact with a checkout (click a payment button), Lemon Squeezy may set session cookies on their domain to process your payment and maintain checkout state.

These cookies are:

  • Set by app.lemonsqueezy.com, not by ariatrust.org
  • Used solely to facilitate the payment transaction
  • Not used by ARIA for tracking, analytics, or marketing
  • Governed by Lemon Squeezy's Privacy Policy

No Lemon Squeezy cookies are set if you do not click a checkout button.

4. How to Control Cookies

Browser settings. You can manage, restrict, or delete cookies through your browser settings. Below are links to cookie management instructions for common browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

Effect of blocking cookies. Because we only use one essential session cookie, blocking or clearing cookies from ariatrust.org will log you out of the Dashboard. You can continue to use public pages (docs, pricing, landing page) without any cookies. Blocking third-party cookies will prevent the Lemon Squeezy checkout overlay from functioning; in that case, checkout will open in a new tab instead.

Do Not Track. Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. Since ARIA does not perform any cross-site tracking, DNT has no additional effect — we do not track you regardless of this setting.
