Acceptable Use
Last updated: May 27, 2026  ·  Effective: May 27, 2026

Prohibited uses

Do not use ARIA for illegal, harmful, or deceptive activities. Examples include:

  • Hacking, unauthorized access, or distributing malware.
  • Fraud, money laundering, or other criminal conduct.
  • Non-consensual surveillance or harvesting personal data without consent.
  • Generating false audit trails, impersonation, or fabricating events.
  • Using the service to send spam or unsolicited bulk communications.

Compliance requirements

You must follow applicable laws and industry standards. Keep API keys secure, declare accurate agent scopes, and provide truthful event data. Rate limits and technical constraints must be respected.

Enforcement

We enforce this policy using a graduated approach: warnings, temporary suspension, and permanent termination for severe or repeated violations. Serious illegal activity may be reported to authorities.
